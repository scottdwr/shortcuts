Routinehub Stats Widget

Is a shortcut to download a widget that displays your routinehub stats right on your Home Screen. The widget displays your amount of shortcuts posted, your total number of downloads and your average downloads per shortcuts.

Customizable

  • Background can be a solid or gradient colour
  • All text fields can be coloured, aligned and sized
  • Space of different sizes can be put between text
  • Can choose to reveal or hide certain things

It is also very easy to use
