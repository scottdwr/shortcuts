Fancy Theme Maker

Make custom icons from your downloaded theme

Author: @robric18

Development Tools

Do you have a folder of custom icons you purchased or downloaded online that you want to convert into Home Screen icons that load without having to open shortcuts?

Then Download Fancy Theme Maker. With this shortcut you select all your custom icons at once and the shortcut will traverse through each icon asking which app you want to associate with the icon. Once you are done, it will create a custom profile containing all the icons.
