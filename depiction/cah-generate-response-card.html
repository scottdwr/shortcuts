CAH Generate Response Card

For playing cards against humanity over whatsapp/iMessage

Author: @entee

Games

For playing Cards Against Humanity by text/whatsapp/email whatever. These Shortcuts generate images of Cards against humanity cards for you to deal out to people via text/what’s app.

  • Shuffling: The lines that are used for the cards are pre-shuffled, you don’t want them randomly sent otherwise 2 people could end up with the same card, so cards are generated in the shuffled order.
  • Version: This is from the UK v2 set, you can change if you want don’t forget to shuffle the lines, and set the number of cards.

There are 2 Shortcuts Prompt Cards & Response Cards, response Cards lets you generate more than 1 at a time so eg you can deal 5 to a person at the start of the game.

Playing

You are the dealer. Send everyone their response cards by separate message. Send the Prompt cards to a group message.

Resources for tailoring the Cards

Shuffler to shuffle any lists of cards https://onlinerandomtools.com/shuffle-lines

List of prompts and responses you can addd. https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1lsy7lIwBe-DWOi2PALZPf5DgXHx9MEvKfRw1GaWQkzg/edit#gid=10
